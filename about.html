Bars in Capitol Hill

One student offers their perspective on the bar scene

Bars in capitol hill have a few things in common: there are plenty of options and they stay open well into the morning hours.


One of my favorite bars in Capitol hill was the Garage right on Broadway. What I liked about Garage was the long list of drink options and the food menu, and they had pool tables for entertainment. With friends living nearby,I used to spend most of my weekends nights in Capitol Hill. Our group would start our night at Garage bar and at midnight we would walk down the street to a place called Q nightclub. The bar there would close around 3am.


The great thing about the neighborhood was that we could park in one spot and visit 2 or 3 bars within walking distance of each other. The area has many paid parking spots including the Seattle Central College parking lot that turns into a public paid parking on the weekend for visitors.
